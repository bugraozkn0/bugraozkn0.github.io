About Me

Hi! I have over 5 years of experience in game development, focusing on Unity and C#. I’ve worked on a variety of projects, from casual mobile games to complex systems, always aiming to create engaging and polished experiences. I actively participate in game jams, collaborating with other developers and pushing creative boundaries under tight deadlines. In the past two years, I’ve also taken on producer roles, managing teams and guiding projects from start to finish. I’m deeply involved in the game development community, constantly sharing knowledge and learning from others. With a background in sound design, music production and visual design, I strive to create cohesive and immersive game worlds that resonate with players.
